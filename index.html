Additional Conservation Tips

  • Leftover Water: Dump cooking water, ice cubes, and other leftover water in the back yard, never down the drain
  • Hand-washing Laundry: If needed, wash small items by hand and hang to dry outside
  • Report Leaks Immediately: Any running toilets, dripping faucets, or plumbing issues should be reported right away
  • Emergency Contact: For urgent water or septic issues, contact your rental host immediately
